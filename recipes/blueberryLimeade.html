Blueberry Limeade

Description

Refreshing blueberry limeade is prepared with fresh blueberries, lime juice, and sugar. Serve cold over ice. Stir well before serving.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • ⅓ cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 6 cups water, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Blend blueberries, sugar, lime juice, and 1 cup water together in a blender; pour into a pitcher. Add remaining water and stir.
